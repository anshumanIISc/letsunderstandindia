Let's Understand India

Ask The Question Change The Nation

Do rapes and terror attacks make you angry ? Does corruption and pollution frustrate you ? Do you want things to change but don't know how you can contribute ? This book gives you the chance to do your bit for the nation. It explains what has led to the present state of affairs and what needs to be done to change it. You can help the nation by asking a question, a very impactful question. " Do our leaders have a system to shield them from internal and external negative forces ?" Leaders give direction to the society, their real fight is against the negative traits of greed, dishonesty and self centered thinking. If they consistently win this fight, the society and the nation will surely progress. India was prosperous because we answered this question correctly for ages before foreigners invaded our land. We achieved prosperity without harming the environment, something which the modern civilization has failed to achieve. We have always been proud of our culture, now is the time to rediscover our strong roots and bring back our glorious past.
